What You'll Learn!

Through an IT Graduate Apprenticeship you are given the opportunity to gain experience in different areas of SSE's network business. A four year placement plan will provided to you and it will work cohesively with your university modules. Hone your skills through being involved and working on innovative projects with critical national infrastructure. The different areas of placement can include working in the Real Time System's team, Cyber Security, Cartography and more. You will be attending Dundee University's Graduate Apprenticeship courses, designed with industrial experience to help employees and apprentices learn at degree level. They offer practical, work-based learning in a range of subjects to meet business goals. Learn while you work! Enjoy...
